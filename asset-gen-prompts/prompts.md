# Image Generation Prompts — Burger Ingredient Assets

## Overview

30 ingredients total across 5 prompts. Each prompt produces **one image** containing **6 ingredients** arranged in a **3-column × 2-row grid**. All images share the same visual language: photorealistic food photography, isometric perspective (roughly 30° elevation, slightly in front and above), ingredients isolated as flat horizontal layers on a pure near-black background (#09090b), soft diffused overhead key light from the upper-left, subtle warm specular highlights, thin natural drop shadow beneath each ingredient. No text, no labels, no decorative borders.

The perspective throughout should feel like a cross-section ingredient "exploded view" — each ingredient rests flat, filling roughly 80% of its grid cell, showing its top surface prominently and a thin front edge that communicates thickness.
